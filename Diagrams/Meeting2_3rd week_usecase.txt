Player:
 
1      Player can move in any direction.
2      Player can fall.
3      Player can burst the balloon of the fellow player.
4      Player can burst the answer balloon.
5      Player is reborn when bursts the wrong answer.
6      Player is reborn when attacked first.
7      Player is reborn when you fall in the sea.
8      Player dies when falls in the sea 2nd time.
9       Player dies when attacked 2nd time.
10   Player wins
11   Player loses

 
System:
1      Maintains timer
2      Produces the question
3      Release answer balloons from the pipe.
4      Check the correct answer with the pricked balloon.
5      Track status of the player
